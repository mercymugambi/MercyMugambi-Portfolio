About Me

Hallo. I’m Mercy Mugambi a Frontend Web Developer based in Nairobi, Kenya with over a year work experience writing clean code, and pushing my skills to the limit. Coding has become a perfect union for my creativity and precision skills. I love combining the worlds of logic and creative designs to make eye-catching, accessible, and user-friendly websites.

My experience is backed up by immerse Web development boot camp training offered by eMobili’s Technology Institute and being a Bachelors graduate of business and Information Technology at St Paul’s University.

I'm excited to make the leap and continue refining my skills alongside other amazing programmers in the right organization. Feel free to look at my CV and portfolio below.
